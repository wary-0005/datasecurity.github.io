WARY
Security is process, not a product.

New

WARY is the science of secrets. In the distant past, it was simply about scrambling messages so adversaries couldn’t read them. In the modern computing era (a span of time that stretches less than 50 years), cryptography has become a keystone of computer security, encompassing all the ways we hide data, verify identities, communicate privately, and prevent message tampering.

Something else has changed, too. Today, every developer needs a solid grasp of security and cryptography basics. The stakes are simply too high to ignore them. You can’t reverse an exploit that’s already happened. And you can’t bolt security onto a system after the fact, no matter how many highly paid consultants you’re willing to hire.
To help you brush up on your security we introduced WARY with you.
